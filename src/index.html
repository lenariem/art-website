FAQ

We will answer any of your questions

How long does it take to produce an order?

The lead time for an order depends on three factors: the complexity of the painting, the style of writing and the size. The average order fulfillment time is 7 days excluding delivery time. In some cases, at the request of the customer, the period can be reduced to 5 days.

Note: If you decide to change something in the picture, make adjustments - this may take additional time, which is not taken into account in the agreed deadline.

Will I be able to see the painting before I receive it?

Yes. When ready, we send a digital photo of your painting by email so that you can, if necessary, make your adjustments and wishes, if any. You can also see the painting “live” in our showroom. Report this to your manager.

What format should the photographs be?

We work with all known image formats! Through the site you can upload JPEG, TIFF, PNG up to 50 megabytes in size. You must own the copyright or have permission to use the image. If you have a different image format (RAW, PDF), then you can use an FTP uploader. If you would like a photo taken from a paper photograph (not digital), send it to us and we will scan, process and return the photo with your order!

Can I give special instructions to the artist?

When you place your order on the site, you can add your comments in a special text field. You can also: combine objects from different photos into one or delete objects from a photo, you can change the background, change clothes, outfits, make a color painting from a black and white photo, and also order a painting from a damaged or old photo.
